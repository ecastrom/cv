## Edgar O. Castro Mendez

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at the School of Social Sciences and Government at Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Monterrey, Mexico.

My research lies in applied microeconomics, with a focus on public economics and market interventions. I use laboratory and field experiments, together with observational data, to study real-world economic problems and derive policy-relevant insights.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the Interdisciplinary Center for Economic Science at George Mason University in December 2023.
